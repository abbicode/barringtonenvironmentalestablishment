BEE ECO-FRIENDLY!

Barrington Environmental Establishment

The Barrington Environmental Establishment, or BEE, is a student-led Barrington, Rhode Island-based organization aiming to spread environmental awareness in the East Bay.

As members of our community, we have a responsibility to protect our planet. We’re aiming to BEE the change.

What We Do

The BEE aims to make local change through monthly events, a biweekly newsletter, and community activism, aiming to spread awareness and make change, all supported by community members. View featured blog posts below or view a full list here. Similarly, find a full archive of past events here, with an archive of our most recent annual tree sale here.

Why We Do It

  • We’re in the middle of a climate crisis. According to the United Nations, risks of climate change include intense droughts, severe fires, rising sea levels, melting polar ice, catastrophic storms and declining biodiversity. We'll be seeing impact on both global and local scales. That's why we aim to make local change. Through community actions, we hope to spread awareness and positive change which work on a grassroots level.
